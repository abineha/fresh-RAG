Cheese filling to make peynirli börek

Ingredients

1 cup cottage cheese

1 cup feta, crumbled

1 egg, beaten

1 Tbsp parsley or dill, chopped

¼ tsp pepper

Procedure

Mix the feta into the cottage cheese by mashing together with a fork.

Add the beaten egg, chopped herbs and pepper, and stir until just mixed.

Place in a plastic covered container and refrigerate until ready to use.

See Börek recipe for further instructions.
