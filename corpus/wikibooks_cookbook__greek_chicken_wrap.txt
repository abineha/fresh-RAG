Ingredients

2 cups shredded cooked chicken

2 ½ cups crumbled feta cheese

½ cup Greek Yogurt Sauce

½ cup fresh oregano, finely chopped

4 flour tortillas

Olive oil

Procedure

Combine feta, chicken, yogurt sauce, and oregano. Set aside.

Brush one side of each tortilla with olive oil. Add ¼ of chicken mixture and roll the longer side up.

Repeat until all tortillas have been rolled.

Sprinkle with additional chopped oregano, and serve cold as a snack.

Notes, tips, and variations

You could also do this with pitas for a more traditional version.
