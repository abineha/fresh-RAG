Ingredients

Red tomatoes, chopped

Cucumber, sliced into rounds

Red or white onions, sliced into half-moons

Green bell peppers, cut into small pieces

Greek olives

Extra-virgin or virgin olive oil

Vinegar or lemon juice

Salt

Feta cheese

Oregano

Procedure

Combine tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, peppers, and olives in a deep bowl.

Add enough olive oil, vinegar or lemon juice, and salt to taste. Toss the ingredients for half a minute.

Place a feta cheese slice on top and season with a splash of olive oil.

Top with a sprig of oregano if desired.
