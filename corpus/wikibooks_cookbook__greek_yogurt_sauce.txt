This yogurt sauce is good for gyros, as a grilling baste for lamb and pork, and a dipping sauce for chips.

Ingredients

1 ½ cups plain yogurt

¼ cup freshly-squeezed lemon juice

¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil

2 tbsp dried oregano

1 tbsp minced garlic

Salt to taste

Freshly-ground black pepper to taste

¼ ea. small red onion, finely diced

1 ½ tsp fresh rosemary, finely chopped

2 tbsp flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped

Procedure

Pulse all ingredients in a food processor until relatively smooth.

Pour into your dipping bowl, and serve with a Greek-style dish.
