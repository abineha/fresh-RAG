Tzatziki is a yogurt sauce that's great over gyros, pitas, salads, lamb, as a dip, or for anything you'd like a cool tasty sauce on.

Ingredients

1½ cups yogurt

4 cloves garlic, minced

1 tablespoon dill (sub. ¼ tablespoon of tarragon if no dill is available)

2 tablespoons olive oil

4 tablespoons lemon juice (optional)

2 small cucumbers, diced

Salt

White pepper, to taste (optional)

Procedure

Drain yogurt in a sieve or a cheesecloth for about 3 hours prior to using it.

Drain the cucumber and salt it to further remove moisture.

Combine yogurt, garlic, olive oil, and lemon juice in a bowl and cover and refrigerate for 1 hour or more.

After refrigerating, add dill and cucumber into bowl and mix with a whisk until smooth.

Add pepper and salt to taste.
